Game Info

  • Money Frenzy is a clicker-tycoon game featuring dynamic, and multi-functional events, inspired by Cookie Clicker and BitLife.
  • The goal is to become the world’s first trillionaire by purchasing properties and building your income from the ground up.
  • However, life doesn’t make it easy, random events, both fortunate and disastrous, constantly challenge your rise to wealth, much like in the real world.
  • Test your resilience against the daily grind of building wealth, will you rise to the top, or fall like the rest?
